Field Leadership Training that Works!

Effective Leadership Skills for Construction Field Leaders provides an easy, effective, and affordable way to help new and experienced construction field leaders develop the key leadership skills needed to plan projects and supervise crews.


This innovative program from ATP incorporates job-site leadership animations, real-world case studies, critical-thinking exercises, and other activities to instill key leadership skills used in the field. The printed training manual, combined with its online learner resources, explains the role of effective field leadership in maximizing profits.

The comprehensive online instructor resources include leadership animation guides and case study guides to facilitate instructor-led discussions, plus detailed instructional plans and PowerPoint® presentations for an easy-to-implement, turnkey training package.
