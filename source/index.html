Welding Skills—5th Edition

Welding Skills is an industry-leading textbook designed to provide the essential knowledge and skill-building exercises necessary to succeed in today’s welding industry. This highly readable textbook uses hundreds of full-color illustrations and application photos to illustrate key concepts, while easy-to-use charts consolidate key information for quick reference.

  • Comprehensive coverage of welding safety based on ANSI/AWS Z49.1
  • Expanded coverage of pipe welding, including modifi ed GMAW-S
  • Reorganized and expanded coverage of essential metallurgy and weldability of ferrous and nonferrous metals
  • Updated coverage of weld evaluation and testing methods Use of standard AWS terminology throughout
