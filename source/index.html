Welding Skills—5th Edition

Welding Skills is an industry-leading textbook that provides the essential welding knowledge and skill-building exercises necessary to succeed in today’s welding industry. Hundreds of full-color illustrations and application photos illustrate key concepts and easy-to-use charts consolidate key information for quick reference.

  • Comprehensive coverage of welding safety based on ANSI/AWS Z49.1
  • Expanded coverage of pipe welding, including modified GMAW-S
  • Reorganized and expanded coverage of essential metallurgy and weldability of ferrous and nonferrous metals
  • Updated coverage of weld evaluation and testing methods
