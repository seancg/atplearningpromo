Culinary Arts Principles and Applications
Third Edition

by Michael J. McGreal, MEd, CEC, CCE, CHE, FMP, CHA, MCFE

Every facet of the third edition of Culinary Arts Principles and Applications and its companion study guide has been developed to engage learners, enhance their classroom-lecture experience, and support hands-on learning, whether it takes place in a student-run restaurant or during lab activities. Comprehensive online learner and instructor resources round out this integrated platform for teaching the fundamentals of professional cooking.

The new resource package helps learners:

  • Visualize key concepts through more than 1100 illustrations
  • Practice essential culinary techniques depicted in 134 step-by-step procedures
  • Master foundational cooking techniques using 189 skill-based recipes
  • Apply knowledge and skills using 384 Performance Checks
  • Follow current industry standards through product identification, including updated beef and pork cuts
  • Interact with learning tools provided in the complimentary online learner resources

Also Available

The Culinary Arts Principles and Applications Study Guide reinforces the concepts presented in the textbook using true-false, multiple choice, completion, and matching review questions. A variety of activities helps students verify their understanding of fundamental concepts and their applications.
