Occupational Safety & Health, 1st Edition

Occupational Safety and Health provides an overview of potential workplace hazards, necessary safety practices, and how various processes need to be managed in order to maintain a safe workplace. This resource is designed for use in introductory safety courses and by professionals who want to advance in the field as well as individuals who need to understand and implement safety programs. Appropriate safety regulations and laws, hazard recognition, and personal protective equipment are addressed. This learning resource is your perfect start to developing an internal safety program.

  • Updated Rules on Reporting Injuries
  • Case Studies on Safety Practices
  • Links to OSHA and Other Online Safety Resources
