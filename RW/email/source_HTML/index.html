WELL AP® Exam Preparation Guide – First Edition

Credentials for a WELL Built Environment

The WELL Building Standard is a health-centered approach to building design and construction. The WELL AP credential signifies both knowledge of human health and wellness in the built environment and specialization in the standard. This guide emphasizes mastery of the standard, the seven WELL Concepts, all of the features and their requirements, and the WELL certification process.
