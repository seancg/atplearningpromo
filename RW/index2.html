Residential Wiring and Smart Home Technology

Residential Wiring and Smart Home Technology focuses on the principles, installation, maintenance, and enhancement of residential electrical systems. This edition provides expanded material on utility power generation and distribution, electrical protection and devices, tools and test instruments, and electrical safety and NEC® installation guidelines. This edition also introduces smart home technology, including smart infrastructure, automation systems and devices, security and fire alarm systems, energy management, and lifestyle applications.
